About Eco Exchange

Our mission to create a sustainable sharing economy

Eco Exchange was founded in 2023 with a simple vision: to create a platform where people could exchange goods sustainably, reducing waste and building community connections.

We believe that every item shared is one less item in a landfill, and every exchange helps build a more sustainable future for our planet.
